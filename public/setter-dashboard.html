Call Scripts
Cold Call
Opener
For prospects who have not received an email yet - fresh outreach.
"Hey, is this [name]? Hey, my name's [your name] - I'm reaching out on behalf of Pulseforge, we work with local businesses in the area on one specific thing: bringing in new customers consistently without adding more to your plate. Is that something that's on your radar right now for [business name]?"
If yes: "Perfect - that's exactly what we help with. Can I grab 20 minutes of your time this week to show you what it looks like?"
If no/not right now: "Totally fair - can I ask, is it more a timing thing or just not a priority right now?"
Transition to Booking
"The way it works is Jacob - he's the founder - does a quick 20-minute walkthrough, no pitch, just shows you what the system actually does for a business like yours. He has a couple spots open this week. Would [Tuesday] or [Wednesday] work?"
Close
"Perfect — here's Jacob's calendar link, you can grab a time right now: calendly.com/jacob-gopulseforge/new-meeting. Takes 30 seconds. Want me to stay on while you grab a spot?"
If They Ask What You Do
"Basically we build an automated outreach system that finds local prospects, reaches out on your behalf, and keeps your business visible between jobs - all running in the background while you focus on the actual work. Most owners we talk to are great at what they do but don't have time to market consistently. That's the gap we fill."

Handling Guides

Offer Jacob's calendar so he can walk through the live dashboard with real pipeline data.

FAQ
Common Questions
How do I log a call?
Use Log Call on any lead, choose the disposition, add notes and an optional callback or duration, then save.
What if a lead has no phone number?
Click "Enrich Phone" on the lead card. The system will try to find a number automatically.
What do I do with a warm reply?
Flag it immediately using the hot flag button and text Jacob at jacob@gopulseforge.com.
When do I get paid?
Same week a call shows or a client closes. Jacob will reach out to confirm and send payment.
Who do I contact with questions?
Jacob Maynard - jacob@gopulseforge.com or text directly.
