## Disposition rules

1. **Book** only when the prospect manages multiple units/properties and confirms an outside-cleaner or backup-coverage need.
2. **Nurture** when the prospect is in territory and has a relevant portfolio but says its current cleaner is stable; request permission for a one-time backup quote.
3. **Disqualify** companies that state they manage only long-term unfurnished rentals and have no make-ready/cleaning vendor need; do not recycle as STR leads.
4. Record the decision-maker, managed-property count, current cleaning setup, gap type, and next step after every call.

## Excluded

- Blue Door Living: explicitly does not manage short-term, mid-term, or furnished rentals.
- Businesses headquartered only in excluded markets (Concord, Nashua, Derry, Merrimack, Seacoast, and the Lakes Region) without verified service in the Anchor territory.
- Single-property hobby hosts and Airbnb guest messaging.
